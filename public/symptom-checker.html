3D Symptom Checker

The 3D Symptom Checker by Know Your Body is an innovative tool designed to help you identify areas of discomfort in your body in a visual and precise way. Using an interactive 3D model, you can pinpoint any area causing pain or discomfort and obtain relevant information about possible causes.

This tool is intuitive and easy to use, allowing you to explore different parts of the body and see how symptoms may be related to various medical conditions. It is a helpful first step in better understanding your symptoms and deciding whether you need to seek professional medical attention.

Additionally, the 3D Symptom Checker is integrated with our medical knowledge base and educational resources, offering a more comprehensive and contextualized view of your health.
