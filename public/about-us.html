About Us

Know Your Body is an innovative project created by university students with the goal of providing easy and intuitive access to medical information from anywhere, free of charge.

Our web tool uses a revolutionary 3D model that allows users to identify areas of discomfort in their body, facilitating a clearer understanding of potential health issues. Our purpose is to empower people to make informed decisions about their well-being with the best resources available.

United by a passion for health and technology, our diverse team works to develop an accessible and effective tool for everyone. Discover how we are transforming access to healthcare!
